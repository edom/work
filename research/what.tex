\index{argmin}%
Define \(m = \arg\min_{x \in X}(f~x)\) iff \(\forall x \in X : f~m \le f~x\).

\index{min}%
Define \(\min_{x \in X}(f~x) = f~(\arg\min_{x \in X}(f~x))\).
