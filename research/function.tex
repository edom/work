\section{Fractional iterate}

Generalize \(f^p\) for real \(p\).
\begin{align*}
    f^2 &= f \circ f
    \\
    f^{1/2} \circ f^{1/2} &= f
\end{align*}

If \(f~x = x \uparrow a\) then \(f~(f~x) = (x \uparrow a) \uparrow a\).

What is a \(g\) that satisfies \(g~(g~x) = x^a\)?

What does \(d~(p \to f^p)\) even mean?
The \(d\) is differential operator.
